# Chop Now Documentation

## About Chop Now
Chop Now is a food e-commerce platform that delivers fresh produce and food items directly to customers. The platform focuses on affordability, accessibility, and social impact by offering special benefits to students, NYSC members, and people in need.

Chop Now partners with local farmers and food vendors to ensure fresh products, fair pricing, and reliable delivery.

---

## Products Offered
Chop Now sells a variety of food items, including:
- Fresh vegetables such as tomatoes, peppers, onions, and leafy greens
- Fresh fruits including bananas, oranges, apples, and pineapples
- Grains and staples such as rice, beans, garri, yam, and plantain
- Protein sources including eggs, chicken, fish, and beef
- Cooking essentials such as cooking oil, spices, and seasoning cubes

Product availability may vary depending on location and season.

---

## Ordering Process
Customers can place orders by:
1. Selecting food items
2. Adding items to the shopping cart
3. Choosing a delivery address
4. Selecting a delivery time slot
5. Completing payment

Order confirmation is sent after a successful checkout.

---

## Delivery Policy
- Standard delivery is completed within 24 hours
- Same-day delivery may be available in selected areas
- Delivery fees depend on distance and order size
- Customers are notified in case of delays caused by weather or supply issues

---

## Promotions and Discounts

### Student Discounts
Verified secondary school and university students are eligible for student discounts. A valid student ID is required for verification. Discount rates may change depending on active promotions.

### NYSC Member Benefits
Active NYSC members can access exclusive promotions. Verification requires an NYSC ID or call-up number. Discounts may apply to selected food bundles.

### Support for People in Need
Chop Now operates food support programs for low-income households, elderly individuals, and people facing food insecurity. These programs may include discounted food packages, free food deliveries, and partnerships with NGOs or community groups.

---

## Payment Methods
Supported payment options include:
- Debit cards
- Bank transfers
- Mobile wallets where available
- Cash on delivery in limited locations

Payment availability depends on customer location.

---

## Refunds and Cancellations
Orders can be cancelled before dispatch. Refunds are processed within 3 to 5 business days. Perishable items may not be eligible for refunds after delivery. Issues with damaged or missing items must be reported within 24 hours.

---

## Account Verification
Account verification is required to access certain benefits:
- Students must upload a valid student ID
- NYSC members must provide NYSC identification

Verification is typically completed within 24 to 48 hours. Unverified users can still place orders but may not receive special discounts.

---

## Customer Support
Customers can contact Chop Now support through:
- In-app chat
- Email support
- Phone support during business hours

Support assists with order tracking, refunds, discount eligibility, and account verification.
